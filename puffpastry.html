Galette des Rois

King’s cake

A rich and creamy almond paste is hidden between two puff pastry doughs.

Fun fact: a porcelain piece is hidden inside the cake to be shared with family, friends or colleagues! If you find it in your slice, you’re the king!

Filling options:

  • Peanut, pecan, hazelnuts, coconut instead of almonds
  • Chocolate
  • Apple, Pear puree with a touch of cinnamon or ginger

Chaussons aux pommes

Apple empañadas

Each individual cake is filled with apple sauce and baked in the oven.

Filling options:

  • All the fruit purees
  • Additionnal pieces: whole almonds, hazelnuts, raisins, etc.
